About Us

Our goal is to meet the unique needs of highly motivated executives and internationally minded individuals who want to interact, communicate, engage, and negotiate with native english speakers within an english speaking environment. Our lesson plans are custom tailored to fit each clients needs, and to ensure an elegant learning experience.

Our Founder

Terry Posylek

With over 40 years of both teaching and executive experience, I have made it my mission to help non-English speaking executives effectively communicate in English speaking environments. I started this mission with a simple underlying motivation to mentor and encourage open-minded English language learning in an effort to help unique individuals hone their English communication skills.
