About Us

Our goal is to meet the unique needs of highly motivated executives and internationally minded individuals who want to interact, communicate, engage, and negotiate within an English speaking environment. Our lesson plans are custom tailored to fit each client's needs, and to ensure an elegant learning experience.

Meaningful conversation based on genuine human connection is my personal mission. I collaborate closely with a select group of clients to provide customized English conversation and learning. I expect that my client's will approach and engage in every one-on-one session by applying the Bespoke – ESL 3 step model: Contemplate – Articulate – Navigate.

With over 40 years teaching and business experience, my simple underlying motivation, and aim is to mentor, facilitate, and encourage meaningful conversation based on genuine human connection. I sincerely look forward to working with you.
